◆ Stage 05 · About

Why we set the table.

— 01

Operators, not pundits.

Every guest has shipped something hard. We skip the thesis tour and get into the craft — the decisions, the dead ends, the un-sexy middle.

— 02

The long conversation.

No hot takes. One hour minimum, often longer. Space for nuance, for being wrong out loud, for the second-order thought.

— 03

A real table.

The podcast is the public record. The dinners, padel games and salons are where the community actually forms.
